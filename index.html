About Us

Frontend Simplified is a community of developers who are passionate about frontend development. We provide resources, tutorials, and a supportive community to help you learn and grow as a frontend developer.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make frontend development accessible to everyone. We believe that with the right resources and support, anyone can become a skilled frontend developer.

Frontend simplified teaches absolute beinners how to develop skills they can use to become succesful in the development industry. With our extinsive knowlage and learning structure, combined with your dedication. You can be developers within just a few short months!
